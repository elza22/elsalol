LIONEL MESSI

Lionel Messi is considered the greatest football player ever. His amazing skills, scoring lots of goals, and smart playing on the field make him stand out. With 8 Ballon D`Ors and in total 45 trophies,Messi is seen as the best in the history of football. His special mix of quick moves, creativity, and always playing at a high level has made a big impact.

- Birthdate:June 24, 1987 - Birthplace:Rosario, Argentina - Position:Forward - Main Clubs:FC Barcelona (2004-2021), Paris Saint-Germain (2021-2023), Inter Miami (since 2023)
